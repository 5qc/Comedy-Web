The Scale

The Scale is an unofficial fire scale that is used to measure the intensity of fires. These fires are based off of the cost it did. So even if a fire claimed 200,000 acres of land but only $20 million of damages was done, it would still be classified as a Fire III.
(All fires are measured in USD (United States dollars)

I = $0 - $99k
II = $100k - $999k
III = $1M - $9M
IV = $10M - $49M
V = $50M - $99M
VI = $100M - $500M
VII = $1B - $2.4B
VIII = $2.5B - $4B
IX = $5B - $10B
X = $10B+
